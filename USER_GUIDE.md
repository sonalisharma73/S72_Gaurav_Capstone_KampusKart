# KampusKart User Guide

## 🎓 Welcome to KampusKart!

Your campus community platform for lost & found items. Never lose track of your belongings again!

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account
1. Visit [KampusKart](https://kampuskart-frontend.netlify.app)
2. Click "Register" in the top navigation
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Name**: Your full name
   - **Email**: Your campus email (recommended)
   - **Password**: At least 6 characters
4. Click "Create Account"

**Alternative**: Use "Sign in with Google" for quick registration

### Step 2: Explore the Platform
- **Home**: Overview and recent items
- **Items**: Browse all lost & found items
- **Profile**: Your personal dashboard

---

## 📦 Reporting Lost Items

### When You Lose Something
1. Click "Items" → "Report Lost/Found Item"
2. Select "Lost" as the type
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: Brief description (e.g., "Black iPhone 13")
   - **Description**: Detailed description
   - **Category**: Choose from 8 categories
   - **Location**: Where you last saw it
   - **Date**: When you last saw it
   - **Contact**: How people can reach you
4. **Add Photo** (optional but recommended)
5. Click "Submit"

### Tips for Better Results
- ✅ Be specific in your description
- ✅ Include unique identifying features
- ✅ Add a clear photo if possible
- ✅ Provide accurate location details
- ✅ Check back regularly for updates

---

## 🔍 Reporting Found Items

### When You Find Something
1. Click "Items" → "Report Lost/Found Item"
2. Select "Found" as the type
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: What you found
   - **Description**: Detailed description
   - **Category**: Item category
   - **Location**: Where you found it
   - **Date**: When you found it
   - **Contact**: How the owner can reach you
4. **Add Photo** to help owner identify
5. Click "Submit"

### Good Samaritan Tips
- ✅ Take a photo but don't show serial numbers publicly
- ✅ Provide a safe meeting location
- ✅ Verify ownership before returning items
- ✅ Consider meeting in public places

---

## 🔍 Searching for Items

### Browse All Items
1. Go to "Items" page
2. Use filters to narrow down:
   - **Category**: Electronics, Clothing, Books, etc.
   - **Type**: Lost or Found
   - **Status**: Open or Resolved
   - **Search**: Keywords in title/description

### Search Tips
- 🔍 Use specific keywords
- 📱 Filter by category for faster results
- 📅 Sort by date to see recent items
- 🔄 Check regularly - new items are added daily

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Is KampusKart free to use?
**A**: Yes! KampusKart is completely free for all students.

### Q: Can I edit my item after posting?
**A**: Yes, you can edit your own items anytime from your profile.

### Q: How do I mark an item as found/returned?
**A**: Edit your item and change the status to "Resolved".

### Q: Can I delete my item?
**A**: Yes, you can delete items from your profile dashboard.

### Q: What if someone claims my item falsely?
**A**: Always verify ownership. Ask for specific details only the real owner would know.

### Q: How long do items stay on the platform?
**A**: Items remain until marked as resolved or deleted by the owner.

### Q: Can I contact multiple people about the same item?
**A**: Yes, but be courteous and update others if you find your item.

### Q: What if I can't upload a photo?
**A**: Photos are optional. Provide a detailed description instead.
